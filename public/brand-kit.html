08 — Token Library

Sacred Geometry Tokens

The lotus petal is the atomic unit of the KODY SUN visual language — a single pointed teardrop rendered as two mirrored quadratic bézier curves. By rotating copies of this base form at precise angular intervals, twelve distinct ceremonial tokens emerge. Each carries its own visual energy, petal density, and use context across the brand system.
